How to raise a ticket?

To raise a new ticket, simply click on the "Submit a Request" link in the navigation bar. Fill out the form with a clear subject and detailed description of your issue, then click submit.

What to do if our ticket is raised but no update?

If you haven't received an update on your ticket, please check your Dashboard. We recommend waiting a business day for a response. If the issue is urgent, you can reply to the existing ticket to add more information and notify our support team.

Why is my withdrawal pending?

Withdrawals can be pending for various reasons, including security checks or bank processing times. Please allow 3-5 business days.

How do I deposit funds?

You can deposit funds by navigating to the "Deposit" section in your account dashboard and choosing your preferred payment method.
